Get Involved

We believe that every individual has the power to drive change. Sail Et' Plastic invites you to join our mission and take meaningful action against plastic pollution. There are numerous ways you can contribute to our cause:


  • Volunteer: Participate in cleanup drives and contribute your time and skills to our initiative.
  • Donate: Help us expand our fleet of plastic-cleaning houseboats and develop advanced recycling technologies.
  • Spread Awareness: Educate your community about the impact of plastic pollution and inspire others to join the fight.
